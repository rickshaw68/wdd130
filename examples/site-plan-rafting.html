Akela's Adventures site plan

John Stewart

Overview

Purpose

"The purpose for a website for "Akela's Adventures" is to highlight the adventures of whitewater rafting, season that we're open supplies and preparation needed, and encourage boys and young men for outdoors skills. Boys and Young Men must be shown that they need to strive for excellence regardless of their experience level. On top of single and two-day ventures, weeklong trips will be advertised as character/skill-building exercises. The primary message being pushed is "Fun through Skill-Building"

Audience

The specific intended audience is for older boys and young men (17-21) who are either in the scouting program, or want a taste of it again(either from being too old or not getting a chance in the first place) to experience the action of an outdoors rafting experience.
