Rocky Mountain Rapids

At Rocky Mountain Rapids, we believe in providing thrilling outdoor experiences that leave our clients wanting more. Our mission is to promote adventure, respect for our environment, and appreciation for nature through our best in industry rafting trips. We are dedicated to safety, fun, and creating memories that last a lifetime.

History

Rocky Mountain Rapids has been bringing enjoyment to visitors since 1986 by founder Rick Shaw. We started out as weekend river guides but quickly took off to what we are today, a must do destination experience for visitors from around the world
