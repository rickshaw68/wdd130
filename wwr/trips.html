Rocky Mountain Rapids -- Our Trips

Idle Package

Our Family Friendly day on the water features family fun for all ages and experiences. This trip will focus on level I and II rapids. Get ready for a relaxing fun float with our fantastic guides.

Rev It Up Package

Our Rev It Up rafting package takes you to the next level. This trip focuses on class II and III rapids. With this trip, you will enjoy the great outdoors while you experience the perfect mix of exciting white water rapids with a little bit of calm river in between to allow you to catch your breath and get ready for the next challenge.

Throttle Package - All The Rapids You Can Handle

Our Throttle package will take experienced rafters into the most challenging parts of the river. Here you will fulfill your need for heart-thumping, exhilarating, exciting, and challenging whitewater thrills. This trip is only for adults with significant experience where you will ride with other experienced rafters to work as a team to conquer all that the river has to offer. This trip is not for the faint of heart. There is no resting on this trip; there will be all the adrenaline-filled excitement that you can handle, and when you are brought back to base camp after your trip, you will be ready for all the rest and relaxation you can handle.
